Privacy Policy

Information

We collect personal information from you, including information about your:

> Name
> Contact Information
> Location
> Computer or Network
> Interactions with us

Collection

We collect this personal information by asking people directly and by using website cookies. Note that a warning will appear to check that you understand the implications of collecting other information. In this case, analytics are well understood and common case.

Purpose

We collect your personal information in order to make reservations and contact guests in the case of a cancellation or change in booking. We also collect analytical information about how the website is used to allow us to improve user experience.

Sharing

We do not share any personal information with third-parties.

Contact

You have the right to ask for a copy of any personal information we hold about you, and to ask for it to be corrected if you think it is wrong. If you would like to ask for a copy of your information, or to have it corrected, please contact us at info@haukairestaurant.co.nz, or 09 401 4019, or 1 Island Street, Kerikeri, Bay of Islands, New Zealand.

Optional Info

Providing some information is optional. If you choose not to enter personal or contact information, we'll be unable to make a reservation or change or cancel a reservation.
